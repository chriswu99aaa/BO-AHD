# research_plan

A skill for writing research plan and excuting the plan.

## Usage

Describe when and how to use this skill.

When you want to make research on the provided paper or a targeted research topic use this skill. When invoke this skill provides the attached paper or provides a resaerch topic. This skill will generate a research plan including the steps in the plan as well as research objectives.

## Steps

1. read the attached paper or search online on the requested domain.
2. based on the mentioned weakness to propose a research plan.
